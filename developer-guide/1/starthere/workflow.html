Get started

  1. Identify training type (know-it, how-to, break-fix)

  2. Identify lab template on the RHDP or ROLE platform for how-to and break-fix type content.

  3. Submit a Request form for type Open Training

  4. Training team will get in touch with you.

What will be the next steps?

  • Develop training and lab

  • Quality check and Publish
